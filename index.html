What it does
Everything your finance close needs
Designed by a CA who has lived the monthly close grind — every feature exists to save real time.
Trial Balance Upload
Upload CSV or Excel. Map your columns once and the tool handles everything — no reformatting needed each month.
MTD & YTD Variance Analysis
Node-level and line-item variance vs. budget — favourable / unfavourable flagged in colour. Toggle columns on or off instantly.
AI Variance Commentary
One click generates a professional narrative summary of key variances — ready to paste into board packs or management decks.
Auto Email Drafting
Emails to variance owners are drafted automatically, with MTD and YTD figures pre-filled. Send at the click of a button.
Threshold-Based Triggers
Set email triggers by variance %, absolute amount, or MIS node. Only material variances generate alerts — no noise.
Contact-Linked TB
Map each GL line to an owner in your Trial Balance. The tool knows who to chase for every variance — automatically.
